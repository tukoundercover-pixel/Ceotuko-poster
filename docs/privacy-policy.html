Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-19

"ceotuko poster" is a personal-use application built and operated solely by its owner (the TikTok/Instagram account holder @ceotuko) to automate publishing of their own original video content to their own social media accounts.

What this app does

The app reads video files chosen by the owner, generates a caption, and publishes the video to the owner's own Instagram and TikTok accounts using each platform's official API. It is not distributed to or used by any other person or account.

Data collected

This app does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data belonging to any third party. It only handles:

  • Video files the owner chooses to upload
  • API access tokens for the owner's own Instagram and TikTok accounts
  • A local log of the owner's own posting history (video name, caption, timestamp)

All of this data is stored locally by the owner or in their own private cloud hosting environment, and is never transmitted to or accessed by any third party other than the Instagram Graph API and TikTok Content Posting API endpoints required to publish the content.

Data retention

Access tokens and logs are retained only for as long as the owner continues to run the app, and can be deleted at any time by the owner.
